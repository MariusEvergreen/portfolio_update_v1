Background

If I could describe my personality with a single word, since the early childhood until now, that'd be: Adventurer.

I am 22yo now and the adventure only seems to begin (or it began a long time ago). I moved from my homecountry to Italy when I was 15yo to become a dental technician. Not quite what I dreamed of but I never knew what exactly I wanted to become. Even after graduating from Highschool, my struggle still thrived. And the main reason was my ambivalent personality. One part being the artistic mind, and the other part being the scientific mind. Trying to choose only one of them I could not find the right path until I understood that I had to create my own one by combining those elements toghether. That lead me to the Front-End Development: a beatiful combination of desing and technology. In addition the lifestyle of a Web Dev was just what i needed: freedom of choice. :)
